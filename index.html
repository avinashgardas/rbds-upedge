About

If we take a closer look at the student life cycle - in 100% of students only 10% of students who appear for placements get into Good - Core companies with a decent package and the rest of them don’t make it!

We can observe that there is a huge gap in between, a lot of factors have added for this gap to be present until today.

How can we bridge this gap!?

We have brought upedge a student development initiative by River Bend Data Solutions Pvt.Ltd partnering with Nextgrid-poland to bring a reformation on how students learn technology and become Industry ready!

How are we different?

We do not teach We Educate! Students, Instead of following the traditional method of teaching students, we want to encourage students in SELF LEARNING through educating and guiding them to explore the options available around them in the market.

With Upedge we want to aid students with our experience with technology and the network we share! So that students can be equipped with all the right things to pursue a great career.

We are going to help students find their passion identify what they love doing and get them to find their dream jobs!
